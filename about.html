02 : About me

I think about how we can utilize swarms of drones to complete human-focused computer vision tasks with extremely high speed and accuracy.

Currently, I'm pursuing a PhD in EECS from MIT, where I study this work. In the past, I've worked on projects ranging from computer vision, virtual reality, embedded systems and robotics at various organizations including IBM, Google, and Lincoln Laboratory.

I'm inspired by the intersection of radiscal technology and real world needs.

I am the co-founder and CTO of Parfait, where we utilize computer vision to size and customize lace wigs for women. I am particularly inspried by the ability to infuse technology into any sector, and the variety of creative ways we can learn from and utilize the influx of data in non-technical domains.
